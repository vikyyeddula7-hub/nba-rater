How Is This Rating Calculated?

The SmashRater OVR combines five factors:

  • ATP Ranking (base) — Rank 1 starts at 90; drops ~0.23 per position
  • Grand Slam Titles — +1.8 OVR per Slam won (up to +9)
  • Career High Bonus — Former top-5 players get a legacy bump
  • Age & Form — Reflected in current ranking trajectory

Ratings range from 55 (rank 150) to 99 (all-time great). Novak Djokovic's 24 Grand Slams push him above his current ranking.
